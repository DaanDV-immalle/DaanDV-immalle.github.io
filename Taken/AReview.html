Dell/Hp docking station review

When I arrived at Fidea, they gave me a full tour of the company. Something that stood out for me was the fact that there was a docking station on every desk. In the beginning I did not really understand why the device was so useful. I asked for further information about the docking stations. They told me every employee has their own laptop. A portable computer is very handy for working at home or to take to a meeting but for the daily work, it’s more comfortable to have a larger screen and a good keyboard and mouse. That is the reason why there is a docking station on every desk. It makes the switch between a small laptop display and a large, good quality monitor just a “click”. That makes this product perfect for people who work on a laptop for their work, it is so incredibly fast. Also internet connection, keyboard, mouse and power for the laptop are provided by the docking station.

At Fidea they have two types of docking stations, a model for HP laptops and one for Dell laptops. They are not compatible with each other, so an HP laptop will not work in a Dell docking station and vice versa. A small downside for me is that a little amount of old docking stations have a problem to connect with the large monitor. You sometimes have to disconnect the cable and put it back in to make it work like it should.

There are not so many good reviews on the internet about the models that they are using at Fidea. I only found some customers at coolblue.be (laptopshop) who share their experience with the docking station. They confirm that it’s super easy to use: no struggles with wires, just a click. They said once you are used to it, you really can not do without it. Overall all the people were very enthusiastic about it and gave the docking station an average rating of 9,1/10.

My overall opinion is that it’s very useful for people who work at a company, have a laptop and sometimes work at home. It saves you a lot of time and the system is very user friendly. From IT specialists to the old cleaning lady, everyone can use it. Of course, you need a model of laptop that is compatible with the model of dockingstation. otherwise it will not work.
