My IT story

This autobiography is about me, Daan De Vry. I really love everything about computers and software. I’m 16 years old and studying at Immaculata Institute Oostmalle. I study IT and I am now in my last year. In this autobiography, I will explain how I started loving computers, what IT means to me and what I’m going to do with my life in the future.

I discovered my passion for PCs back when I was seven. I started to disassemble computers and search for the functions of all the parts. After a few months, I knew very much about the inside of a computer. Then, I started to replace the old parts with better ones. After one year, I had a very powerful gaming computer. When I was 13, I started programming and learned HTML on my own. I made my own simple HTML webpages. They were not perfectly designed, but still good for a 13-year old kid.

I am now at the point that I can’t live without a pc. I do everything with it: programming and designing websites, ordering food online, checking my bank account and more. What I love the most is designing and programming websites. It’s the only thing on which I can focus for hours. I’ve already made a lot of websites: school projects, … I give my work a personal touch. Websites made by me are clean, clear and “Daan flavoured”. I know the basic HTML by heart but I look up the specific things different for every website on the internet. I think I can call myself an expert but of course there is always room to improvement.

After my Immaculata career, I’m going to study something with IT. I think something with web design but I don't know for sure. In my perfect dreams, I would have my own web design and programming business. Other companies would come to me and ask for a website. I also want to expand my basic IT skills so I can help family and friends with all their IT problems. I hope my future will look like this. Then I’m going to be the happiest man on earth.

IT has changed my life a lot. It’s my passion and I love to spend loads of time on IT. But of course I can still make a lot of fun with my friends and make unforgettable moments together.
